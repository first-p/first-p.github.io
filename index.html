Welcome to the Austin Community College Pickleball Club!

Welcome to the newest, most exciting addition to Austin Community College's club roster – the Pickleball Club! Nestled in the heart of the biggest pickleball city in the South, we're here to bring the fun, camaraderie, and competitive spirit of pickleball right to your doorstep.
Pickleball, a sport that's sweeping the nation with its easy-to-learn rules and engaging play, isn't just a game; it's a community builder. It's where you'll find laughter echoing through the courts, where friendly rallies lead to lifelong friendships, and where every serve brings a new opportunity to connect.
Our club is open to everyone, from those who've never held a paddle to seasoned players with pinpoint precision. The beauty of pickleball lies in its inclusivity; it's a sport that welcomes all skill levels, offering a perfect blend of social interaction and competitive play. Whether you're looking to unwind after class, make new friends, or engage in a friendly challenge, there's a place for you here.
Join us to experience the joy of pickleball in a city that embraces the sport like no other. Let's create memories, build connections, and most importantly, have a blast doing it. See you on the court!
